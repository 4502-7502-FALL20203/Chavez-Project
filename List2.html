10 Must-Try Tex-Mex Restaurants in Dallas


Mariano's Hacienda Ranch

6300 Skillman St, Dallas, TX 75231

(214) 619-3888

laharanch.com

My absolute favorite Tex-Mex spot! Located in the Lake Highlands neighborhood and both indoor and outdoor seating this restaurant is a great location. Make sure to try their house margaritas as they are the original creator of the first frozen margarita machine!



Mi Cocina

3232 McKinney Ave Suite 175, Dallas, TX 75204

(214) 600-8044

micocina.com

Mi Conina has multiple locations around DFW making it a must-stop place no matter where you are.



Desperado's

4818 Greenville Ave, Dallas, TX 75206

(214) 363-1850

desperadosrestaurant.com

Desperado’s is a local favorite, it’s owned and operated by 2 brothers who grew up in the Dallas area and built a delicious restaurant using their grandmother's recipes.



La Calle Dolce

415 W Twelfth St, Dallas, TX 75208

(214) 941-4304

lacalledolce-dallas.com

If you're looking for an authentic feel, La Calle Dolce is the place for you! When you step into this restaurant it feels like you're walking into your abuela’s kitchen.



Tumpinamba Cafe

9665 N Central Expy #142, Dallas, TX 75231

(972) 991-8148

tumpinambacafe.com

Tupinamba is a family friendly quick and easy restaurant stop right off the highway and near North Park mall!



Meso Maya

1611 McKinney Ave, Dallas, TX 75202

(214) 484-6555

mesomaya.com

Slightly more upscale, Meso Maya serves delicious Tex-Mex with an Aztec spin.



Gloria's

3715 Greenville Ave, Dallas, TX 75206

(214) 874-0088

gloriascuisine.com

Gloria’s is the perfect spot for a meal before a night on the town or brunch with the whole family!



Tacos Y Mas

5419 Ross Ave, Dallas, TX 75206

(214) 824-8079

tacosymas.com

If you’re looking for good food with a laid-back atmosphere Tacos Y Mas is the place to go! Serving up Mexican street food with a walk-up window.



Las Palmas

2708 Routh St, Dallas, TX 75201

(214) 647-1232

laspalmas.com

Right in the heart of Up-town, Las Palmas is Tex-Mex with a fun atmosphere, and you might even catch a live mariachi band or two!



Fido's Tacos

4611 Columbia Ave Ste 102, Dallas, TX 75226

(214) 915-8862

fidostacosdetrompo.com

Multiple locations of Fido’s can be found around Dallas and all with delicious street tacos and aguas frescas!
